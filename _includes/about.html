About

Coach Gordon is a certified fencing instructor and coach developer based in Toronto, Ontario.

Gordon competed for Canada internationally in the under 20 category, was a private coach for a senior national team fencer, and volunteered to coach for his alma matter.

He went on to introduce hundreds of newcomers to the sport of fencing by launching new fencing programs in five different clubs in the Greater Toronto Area.

Along the way his students have captured numerous regional and national medals from U11 to Sr.
